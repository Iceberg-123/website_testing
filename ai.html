My Thoughts on AI


I'm just going to use this page as a place to talk about my thoughts on AI


My stance on ai is that it depends on what you're doing and why. For example, my goal for this website is to not use AI at all.

For my other projects like the esp-32, AI did most of the code, because i couldnt have achived my goals without it.

I think using AI is a choice that you can make, but you should make it clear that you used it.

If your goal is to learn how to code or you dont know how yet, then you shouldnt use it, but if you know how, and youre using it as a tool, that works.


I'm not even going to try to argue the environmental impacts, i dont think i'm informed enough on the subject.

also im talking about coding with ai mostly here, so with other places to use it, i think just make sure to double-check the info it gives you.
